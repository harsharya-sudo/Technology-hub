Data Science



Data science ki naukri ek aisa field hai jo data ko analyze karka insights aur knowledge prapt karna ke liya focused hai data scientist data ko collect,process, aur analyze karka buisness decisions ko support karna ke liye data-driven solution develop karta hai

Data science ki naukri ke benefits:
1.High demand: data science skills ki high demand hai, aur data scientist ko achi salary milti hai
2.Variety of industries: data science ka application kai industries mai hota hai,jaisa ki finance, healthcare, retail, aur technology.
3.opportunity to work on complex problems:data scientists ko complex problems solve karna ka mauka milta hai, aur yeh field continuosly evolve ho rahi hai
4.Data-driven decision making:data science data-driven decision making ko enable karta hai, jo business outcomes ko improve kar sakta hai

Data science ki naukri ka roles


1.Data scientist:data ko analyze karke insights aur knowledge prapt karna


2.Data analyst:data ko analyze karka reports aur dashboards create karna


3.Data engineer:data pipelines aur architecture design karna


4.Machine learning engineer:machine learning models develop aur deploy karna




Data science ki naukri ke tools:


1.Python libraries:pandas,numpy,scikit-learn

2.R:statistical analysis aur data visualization

3.Sql:database management

4.Data visualization tools:tableau, power bi
